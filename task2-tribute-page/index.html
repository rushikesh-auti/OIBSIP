Biography

Steven Paul “Steve” Jobs was an American information technology entrepreneur and inventor. He was the co-founder, chairman, and CEO of Apple Inc. Jobs is widely recognized as a pioneer of the microcomputer revolution of the 1970s, along with Apple co-founder Steve Wozniak.
